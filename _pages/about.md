I'm a software engineer focusing on perception with cameras and lidars. I earned my B.S. in vehicle engineering from [Beijing Institute of Technology](https://english.bit.edu.cn/) and my M.S. from [RWTH Aachen University](https://www.rwth-aachen.de/cms/~a/root/?lidx=1) in the direction of autonomous driving. My research interests lie in **3d detection**, **segmentation**, **depth estimation**, **stereo matching**, etc.
